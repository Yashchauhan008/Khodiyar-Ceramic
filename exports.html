Global Exports

Quality That Speaks Globally

With over two decades of experience in international trade, we've established ourselves as a leading exporter of premium tiles and sanitary ware. Our products are carefully crafted to meet international quality standards and are exported to more than 30 countries across Europe, Middle East, and North America.

We pride ourselves on combining traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to create products that stand the test of time. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility ensures consistent quality and timely delivery for all our international clients.

Quality Control

International Standards

Every product that leaves our facility undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets international standards. Our quality control process includes advanced testing methods for water absorption, breaking strength, and frost resistance, making our tiles suitable for diverse global climates.

We hold certifications from leading international quality assessment bodies and comply with all major global standards for ceramic tiles and sanitary ware. This commitment to quality has earned us the trust of architects, designers, and builders worldwide.
